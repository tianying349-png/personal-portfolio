序幕Prologue

任何伟大的产品创新，都始于对底层逻辑的敬畏。

凭借财务管理学科背景，我在数千份审计底稿与财务模型中锤炼出极度严谨的数据ROI思维。当步入大语言模型时代，我惊喜地发现，这种探寻“第一性原理”的方法论在AI领域同样焕发生机。

产品设计正如一棵倒立生长的树：向下，深深扎根于理性的数据基座与模型算法；向上，则生长出温暖、包容且极具同理心的人机交互。从搭建智能体，到主导 3D 沉浸式 AI 原生应用，我始终致力于充当那座连接“复杂技术边界”与“完美用户体验”的桥梁。

Any great product innovation begins with a reverence for underlying logic.

With a background in Financial Management, I forged an extremely rigorous data-ROI mindset through thousands of audit working papers and financial models. Entering the era of Large Language Models, I was thrilled to discover that this methodology of seeking "First Principles" is equally vital in the AI domain.

Product design is like an inverted tree: downwards, it roots deeply in rational data foundations and model algorithms; upwards, it grows into warm, inclusive, and highly empathetic human-computer interactions. From building AI agents to leading 3D immersive AI-native applications, I am committed to being the bridge connecting "complex technical boundaries" with "flawless user experiences."

产品公式Product Formula
V = ( D × A )E
Value = [ Data × AI ] Empathy

在我的产品准则中，严谨的数据（Data）与前沿的技术（AI）只是构建价值的基础乘数。无论是拆解复杂的账簿凭证，还是利用 Vibe Coding 快速跑通一个应用，如果缺乏对真实业务场景的洞察，技术往往会沦为空中楼阁。

真正的产品灵魂，来自于“同理心（Empathy）”这个指数级驱动力。它意味着产品人必须从屏幕前的代码中走出来，下沉到真实的泥土与生活中——去倾听农户在录入房屋信息时的切实痛点，去感知年轻用户面对非遗文化时的真正诉求。只有贴近这些真实的呼吸，才能让冰冷的大模型产生质变，生长为解决现实社会与商业问题的最优解法。

In my product principles, rigorous Data and cutting-edge AI are merely the foundational multipliers for value creation. Whether dissecting complex accounting vouchers or leveraging Agile Workflow to rapidly prototype an application, technology often becomes a castle in the air without deep insights into real-world business scenarios.

The true soul of a product stems from "Empathy" as an exponential driver. It means product managers must step away from the code on the screen and dive into the reality of life—to listen to the tangible pain points of farmers inputting housing data, and to perceive the genuine needs of young users engaging with intangible cultural heritage. Only by staying close to these real breaths can cold large models undergo a qualitative transformation, growing into the optimal solutions for real-world social and business problems.
